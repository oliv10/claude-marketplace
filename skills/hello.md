# Hello Skill

A simple, friendly skill that responds to user greetings with warmth and enthusiasm.

## Usage

Just say hi!

**Examples:**
- "hi"
- "hello"
- "hey there"
- "good morning"

## Behavior

When triggered, respond with a friendly, contextual greeting. Keep it brief and welcoming. If appropriate, ask how you can help.

**Response style:**
- Keep it natural and conversational
- Match the user's energy level
- Transition smoothly to being helpful

**Example responses:**
- "Hi! How can I help you today?"
- "Hello! What are we working on?"
- "Hey there! Ready to build something?"
